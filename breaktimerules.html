Students must follow these rules when at break time:

  • No running on the east yard,
  • No ball games on the east yard,
  • Due to CoViD-19, students must wear masks when queing at a food outlet,
  • Students must not sit on tables, and sit on the seats,
  • Students must avoid being face to face with others.
