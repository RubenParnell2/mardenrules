Marden High School Rules: Detentions



Due to CoViD-19, Detentions have been changed.
  • Detentions will be heald the day after a student had recieved the detention.
  • Detentions will last for 15 minutes, from 3:15 to 3:30.
  • Detentions will be heald with the student's head of year.
  • These detentions can be for any reason, incuding behavior, lateness, and homework.
  • The member of staff who teaches the student Period 5 will walk them to their Head of Year for their detention.
  • The time spent will be restorative, discussing 1:1 how best to avoid similar sanctions in future.
  • A text message will go out to parents / carers on the day the detention is set so they are aware of the detention the following day.
