Marden High School operates a One Way System to help reduce overcrowding in corridoors.
When at school, you must follow the one way system, as stated in the student handbook, page 6, point 2.

This also helps everybody move around the school safley, during CoViD-19 times.
2 meter markers have been placed on the floor to help distance people.
