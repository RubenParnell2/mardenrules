Marden High School has a strict uniform policy. To read the full uniform list, read this document.

Uniform can be purchased from Emblematic.

School Shoes:
School shoes are cleary defined as (on page 10 of the student handbook, found here) polishable black school shoes, leather or vinyl, NOT trainers, canvas shoes or any other soft material. There should be no canvas at all on the shoes.

The basic uniform consists of:

  • Blazer with school badge.
  • School clip on tie.
  • Full length black dress trousers.
  • Black knee length pleated or A-line skirt.
  • Black V neck jumper with school logo.
  • White school shirt.
  • Fashion and casual items of clothing are NOT acceptable.


    • Acceptable image of uniform:


    Jewellery:
    As stated in the student handbook, page 10, other than a watch, one signet ring, and one stud earring per ear, no jewellery is allowed.
    Any other form of body piercing is unacceptable in school.
    Students who have piercings must remove all body jewellery before coming to school.
    Also, as stated by Mr. Snape in the 30/04/21 Newsletter, 'Facial peircings should not be worn for school as they constitute a potential health and safety hazard in the busy school environment'.

    Make-Up:
    As stated in the student handbook, page 10, make-up must be minimal and subtle.
    Heavy make-up, false eyelashes and false nails must be removed completely.
    Also, as stated by Mr. Snape in the 30/04/21 Newsletter, 'Nail extenstions should not be worn for school as they constitute a potential health and safety hazard in the busy school environment'.

    PE Uniform:
    You can find the PE uniform on page 11 of the student handbook.
    Currently, due to CoViD-19, students must come into school in their PE kit on the days when they have PE.

    Hair:
    As stated in 'Year 7 Open Evening 2020',
    'We expect that all of our students look smart and ready to learn. We would not encourage patterns to be cut into hair as extreme hairstyles are not appropriate.'
    Also, as stated in the uniform policy, 'Hair must be a natural colour'.

    Face Coverings:
    Due to CoViD-19, students must also wear a face covering when inside the building, unless exempt.
    Exempt people must get a lanyard to apply for this rule.
    Face Coverings must cover the mouth and nose when on.

    CoViD-19 Excuses for Uniform:
    As stated in 'March-2021_-Back-to-School-Protocol-Spring-back-to-school-4', page 3, point 4, you should wear full school uniform, so CoViD-19, is no excuse for this.

    Year 11 are allowed to come in non-uniform.
